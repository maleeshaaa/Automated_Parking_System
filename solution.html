Solutions for Universities


Running on-campus and off-campus parking isn't always straightforward. Each university, like a city, has its own ecology with distinct parking requirements for different sections of the campus. That's why we built a platform that can handle each car park's unique laws as well as the needs of staff, students, visitors, and contractors.


All of our options, from cashless to pre-book parking, are touchless and eliminate the need for Pay and Display devices. You may notify our system ahead of time if you host an open house or graduation, and even offer free or reduced parking. You may also make parking spots more profitable throughout the year by optimizing them.
